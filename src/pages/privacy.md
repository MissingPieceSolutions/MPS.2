# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 2026**

## Introduction

Missing Piece Solutions ("MPS", "we", "us") operates the website at missingpiecesolutions.com. This policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

This is a simple website. We don't run an e-commerce store, we don't serve ads, and we don't track you across the web. The only personal data we collect is what you choose to send us through the contact form.

---

## What We Collect

When you submit the contact form, we collect:

- **Your name** — so we know who we're talking to
- **Your email address** — so we can reply
- **Your company or organisation** (optional) — to understand your context
- **Your message** — what you've told us about your project or enquiry

We do not collect payment information, create user accounts, or gather any data beyond what you voluntarily submit.

---

## How We Use Your Data

We use your contact form submission for one purpose only: to respond to your enquiry.

We will not:

- Add you to a mailing list without your explicit consent
- Share your details with third parties for marketing
- Use your data for automated decision-making or profiling

---

## Third-Party Services

Two third-party services process data on our behalf:

**Formspree** handles contact form submissions. When you submit the form, your data is transmitted to Formspree's servers and forwarded to us by email. Formspree's privacy policy is available at [formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy](https://formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy/).

**Cloudflare** hosts this website. Cloudflare may process network metadata (such as IP addresses) as part of its infrastructure and security services. Cloudflare's privacy policy is available at [cloudflare.com/privacypolicy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).

No other third-party services receive your personal data.

---

## Data Retention

Form submissions are retained in Formspree until we manually delete them. There is no automated retention period. If you'd like your submission deleted, contact us at the address below.

---

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Request access** to any personal data we hold about you
- **Request deletion** of your data

To exercise either right, email us at privacy@missingpiecesolutions.com. We'll respond within a reasonable timeframe.

---

## Cookies

This website does not use cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising.

Cloudflare may set essential security cookies as part of its infrastructure services. These are not used to track your behaviour across sites.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when changes were last made. Continued use of the site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

---
