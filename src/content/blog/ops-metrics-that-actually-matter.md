## Time saved per workflow

Track net hours recovered by replacing manual tasks, including exception handling overhead.

## Lead response latency

Measure first-response time before and after automations and chatbot routing changes.

## Process reliability

Monitor failure rates and rerun volumes to ensure speed gains do not create quality regressions.
