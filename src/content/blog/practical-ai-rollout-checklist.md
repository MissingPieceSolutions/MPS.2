## Start with one measurable workflow

Choose a single repeated process with clear handoff points. Track cycle time and error rate before and after rollout.

## Define owners before building

Assign one technical owner and one business owner. Shared accountability prevents orphaned automations.

## Ship with observability

Set up logs, retries, and alerts on day one so issues are detected early and fixed fast.
