## Challenge

The support team handled repetitive tickets manually, creating slow response times and inconsistent tagging.

## Solution

MPS implemented a support assistant integrated with helpdesk and CRM systems for auto-categorization and queue routing.

## Outcome

Median response time dropped 42%, and manual triage load dropped by 60% within two months.
