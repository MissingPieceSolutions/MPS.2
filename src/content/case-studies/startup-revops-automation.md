## Challenge

Sales and marketing handoffs were manual and inconsistent, causing lead leakage and delayed outreach.

## Solution

MPS deployed enrichment, scoring, and routing workflows tied directly to CRM lifecycle stages.

## Outcome

Qualified meeting volume increased 31% while reducing manual ops workload across GTM teams.
